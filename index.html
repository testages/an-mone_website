A few An&mone tracks.

Gaelic Bread is the first instrumental song made by An&mone and inspired from scottish traditionnal music
Jungle Fever is a tech song in which An sings about a journey in the jungle !
Night Ride is a remix of a previous An&mone track. In this song, An&mone cruise into space aboard their An&mone DS
Tender Touch is a song that will make you groove and groove and groove to the beat
An&mone is a french duo of multi-instrumentalists Benjamin Sykes and Théodore Michel. Their music is a mix of electro-pop, house, and lofi genres among others. Based in Paris, France, sea anemones are one of the sources of inspiration for their art and visuals, adding to the dreamy and surnatural feel that An&mone conveys. An&mone
